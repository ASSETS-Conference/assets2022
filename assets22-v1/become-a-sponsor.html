Become A Sponsor

October 23-26, 2022

Join us in launching this new era of ASSETS and forging the future of disability+technology!

Your contributions help support travel scholarships targeted at students with disabilities, conference events like the Doctoral Consortium for aspiring PhD researchers, and accessibility support services such as sign language interpreters. In exchange, your brand and representatives will be among current and future leaders shaping the outlook of accessible and universal technology. Benefits and options for sponsorships are shown below.

For more information or to sign-up, please contact generalchair-assets22@acm.org.

- Jon E Froehlich, General Chair
