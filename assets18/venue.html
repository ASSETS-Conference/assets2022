Venue

Location

The ASSETS 2018 conference will be held at The Galmont Hotel & Spa (formerly the Radisson Blu Hotel & Spa) in Galway, Ireland.

The Galmont is superbly located in Galway, just a few minutes’ walk from the bustling shops, pubs and restaurants in the heart of the city centre. If you are arriving into Galway by bus or train, the hotel is about three-minutes’ walk from the main train and bus stations.

Venue

The Galmont offers Guest Rooms, Family & Triple Rooms, Level 5 Executive Rooms, and a Penthouse suite.

There is complimentary Wi-Fi internet access throughout the hotel.

The Galmont's Coopers Bar and Lounge offers an excellent meeting place for you to enjoy food and drink while you continue your conversations with other ASSETS delegates outside of the official conference sessions.

The hotel has an award-winning Spa with thermal facilities and luxurious treatments, as well as a fitness centre with an 18-metre indoor pool, jacuzzi, sauna, steam room, gymnasium and outdoor hot tub.

Room Reservations

The hotel is offering a rate of €145/€155 bed and breakfast for ASSETS attendees.

To book this rate for ASSETS 2018:

  • Please make your booking directly from the hotel website
  • Use the Book Now button (normally displayed in the top right-hand corner of the screen)
  • Input your desired dates. Please note that the agreed conference rate is only valid from Saturday to Wednesday, October 20-24 inclusive. Should delegates wish to book dates either side of the conference dates, a separate reservation will be required.
  • Ensure that the “Type of room” field is set to “All rooms”
  • Enter the Promo Code: ASSETS2018

Rooms are subject to availability.

There are a number of reasons why we would like for delegates to stay at the conference hotel.

The Galmont is a very popular hotel in Galway and so we encourage attendees to book with the hotel as early as possible and ideally by September 19, 2018 as after this date, we may need to release some rooms from our room block.
