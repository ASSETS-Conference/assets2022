Registration Overview

Registrations for conferences can be complicated, reflecting an array of options and time-contingent fee structures. ASSETS is no different. Allow us to help make sense of things. We have:

  • Three registration types: ACM member, non-member, and student. If you are not yet an ACM member, it often makes financial sense to become one. See ACM's membership options here.
  • Three registration deadlines that affect pricing: early, regular, late. Generally, it's best to register early to save costs and to help us plan events.
  • Two conference experiences: the in-person conference in Athens or a lighterweight, asynchronous virtual option for those that cannot travel due to COVID.
  • Workshop registrations: an additional virtual workshop registration option for those accepted into a workshop. You must apply depending on the workshop, see the Accepted Workshops page.

Below, we expand on each option and provide pricing.

In-Person Conference

ASSETS'22 is planned primarily as an in-person event with an asynchronous option for individuals to view pre-recorded videos and engage in discussion on Discord. Please see the Attending Overview page for details.

In short, the in-person conference experience will be similar to previous in-person ASSETS conferences such as ASSETS'18 in Galway, Ireland or ASSETS'19 in Pittsburgh, PA. There will be in-person talks, poster and demo sessions, and two evening social events: one at Hotel Titania and one at the Acropolis Museum and Cafe. Lunches will be provided Mon, Oct 24 - Wed, Oct 26. Similar to our virtual attendees, you will also have access to all pre-recorded, captioned content and our Discord server for text-based chats.

At least one author of accepted technical papers, posters and demonstrations, or other artifact-based tracks is expected to travel to Athens to present their work. If traveling to ASSETS is inaccessible to you or presents an insurmountable COVID health/safety risk for you, please email assets22-leadership@acm.org, and we can find ways to accommodate you.

Virtual Option

ASSETS'22 will offer a lightweight virtual option for those authors and attendees that cannot travel to Athens due to COVID health or accessibility concerns. Virtual registrants will be provided access to all pre-recorded, captioned video content for the entire conference program, including full papers and posters/demos, as well as access to our Discord server for asynchronous and synchronous text-based chats. We will also be offering a few "crossover" events between in-person and virtual attendees; see the Attending Overview page for details.

Virtual Workshops

For the first-time in our conference history, we have a workshop track at ASSETS. This year, there are five accepted workshops—see listing on the Accepted Workshops page—and all workshops are virtual.

You must apply to participate in a workshop. Each workshop is being run independently by the respective co-organizers along with support from the ASSETS'22 Workshop Co-chairs, Kyle Montague and Sowmya Somanath.If you are accepted into a workshop, you must register for the workshop track. We hope you will also choose to register for the conference but this is not required. Please email workshops-assets22@acm.org with questions or concerns.
