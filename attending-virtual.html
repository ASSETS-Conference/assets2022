Attending Virtually

October 23-26, 2022

On This Page

For the first time in our conference history, we are offering a lightweight virtual option for ASSETS. Even if you cannot come to Athens, we hope you will engage with our conference content and community! Please see our Attending Overview page for registration options.

Virtual Conference Overview

For the ASSETS'22 virtual experience, we will largely use the text chat platform Discord, which we used successfully during the pandemic when ASSETS was fully virtual (ASSETS'20 and ASSETS'21). We will also be using YouTube and, for two virtual-oriented events, Zoom. Note: none of the ASSETS'22 content will be live-streamed.

Discord will be the place to chat with in-person attendees, to ask and respond to questions about conference content, to get links to ASSETS'22 videos and papers, and more! Once you've registered for the conference on CVENT, you can join us on Discord here.
