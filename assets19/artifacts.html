ASSETS 2019 Artifact Award and Showcase

Sponsored by Microsoft Research

This year, the ASSETS Conference is highlighting submissions that include some component that is accessible to the public. This includes apps, data sets, instructional guides, and commercial products.

During the conference, attendees can vote on their favorite items.

Prizes:

  • First place: $1000
  • Second place: $500
  • Third place: $250

Winners:

  • First places:
    • Clew iOS App
    • X-Ray Android App
  • Third place: CodeJumper

Link to vote for submissions (for conference attendees only). Voting is open until Wednesday at 2 PM.
