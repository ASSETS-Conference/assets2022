The ASSETS '22 Conference will be geofenced for Aira enabling complimentary access. Aira is a service that connects people who are blind or have low vision to highly trained, remotely located human agents. Through an app on your smartphone, Aira delivers instant access to visual information at the touch of a button - enhancing everyday efficiency, engagement, and independence. To find out more about how to use Aira, download the Aira app and try it out.
